About Me:

Hey! I'm a product manager based in Toronto with a passion for building innovative software products. With four years of experience in the industry, I have had the privilege of working on some exciting projects, including Treepz, in the area of shared mobility.

My passion for technology and entrepreneurship has driven my interest in product management. As a product manager, I love the challenge of bringing a product to market by working closely with cross-functional teams and using data to inform product decisions. My goal is to create products that not only meet the needs of users but also drive business growth.
