eSportcave App

Product Type: Mobile App
User Base: 27K +
Company: eSportcave

The Product:

eSportcave App is a skill-based real money mobile game app with over 27k active users. These users are generally mobile game lovers or enthusiasts who enjoy spending their free time playing mobile games but seeking value for the time they spend playing games they love.

In terms of core product features, eSportcave is extremely similar to other other popular mobile gaming apps, offering features like 1 vs 1 & Tournament modes for users to compete against each other in real-time.

While most freemium gaming apps offering similar gaming experience to eSportcave, they most times do no have any value to offer their users and in most cases let you spend money to play games, Due to these factors and other market needs, I was tasked with building a real money mobile gaming app that allows users compete against each other in real-time and earn real cash prizes.

My Role:

As the Product Owner for eSportcave, I work with multidisciplinary team to develop the mobile app from conception to launch. I conducted market research, user demography, market analysis, user interviews, managed user onboarding, oversaw development & A/B Testing phase and worked closely with the product designers and engineering team to ship a successful mobile app.
