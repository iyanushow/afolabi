Treepz App

Product Type: Mobile App
User Base: 1million +
Company: Treepz Inc/p >

The Product:

Treepz is a tech-enabled shared mobility platform that offers commuters predictability, accessibility, and safety in their daily commutes and travels. At Treepz, we are bridging the gap between broken public transport and expensive ride hailing services in Africa. Our goal is o revolutionize the African transportation sector using technology-driven solutions and to create a transportation ecosystem by connecting riders with users.

In terms of core product features, Treepz is similar to other popular Toro, offering features like vehicle rentals and business Treepz for staff of organisations

My Role:

As the Product Owner for Treepz Inc, I work with multidisciplinary team to develop the mobile app from conception to launch. I conducted market research, user story, user demography, market analysis, user interviews, managed user onboarding, oversaw development & A/B Testing phase and worked closely with the product designers and engineering team to ship a successful mobile app.

What I Did:

  • Led daily stand-up meetings, clarified scope, implemented periodic changes, prioritised deliverables, got stakeholders acceptance, monitored for performance and budget.
  • Based feature prioritisation off user feedback and engineering development time
  • Supervised a remote design and engineering team to deploy a new product that was accepted on launch
  • Managed a team of 6 engineers to implement features and increase team performance by implementing Jira ticket management and weekly brainstorming sessions
  • Onboarded 50 early users to stress-test core features
  • A/B tested core features with trial users to determine value of new features based on user feedback


Metrics:

  • 1,000,000 app downloads across 4 African markets
  • Users rated app UI, Features & Functionality of the newly launched app
  • Achieved over 100k completed booking in the first 6 months post launch.

Key-Takeaways:
